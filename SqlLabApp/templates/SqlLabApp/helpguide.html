CREATE A MODULE

1.Upon logging into the system for the first time, a page listing all the modules under an instructor, will be displayed, as shown in figure 1.

2. To create a module, click the "Create new module" button as shown below.

3. Fill in the details of the module in the form. When done, click the "Create new module" button as shown in figure 2.

4. Upon successful creation of a module, the module will be displayed in the modules list page as shown in figure 3.

5. The next step would be to upload the list of students enrolled in that module you just created.


UPLOAD STUDENT LIST

1. On the module listing page, for the module you wish to upload the student list for, click on "View Students", as highlighted in figure 4 below.

3. Upload a text file containing student email addresses, with each line of the text file an email address.

4. The format of a sample text file containing student email addresses is shown in figure 6.

5. After uploading the text file, click the "Upload Student List" button as shown in figure 5.

6. On successful upload of the student list, the names of all students(only those who have registered with SqlLab), and their corresponding email addresses will be shown. Those who have yet to register with SqlLab will be listed as unknown (as shown in Figure 7 below). In this way, you can easily track which student has registered and which has not.
